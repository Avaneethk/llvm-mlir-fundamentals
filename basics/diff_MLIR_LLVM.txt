LLVM provides a fixed low-level SSA IR and a mature optimization/code-generation framework, while MLIR provides an extensible multi-level IR framework where new dialects, types, and operations can be defined. MLIR is typically used to progressively lower high-level domain-specific representations into LLVM IR, which is then optimized and translated into machine code.
